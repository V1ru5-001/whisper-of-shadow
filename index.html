The Story

Dorian has never been alone. For as long as he can remember, something has followed him. Not a voice. Not a memory. A shadow. Watching. Waiting. Whispering in the quiet spaces of his life.

Desperate to uncover the origin of the darkness that has haunted him since childhood, Dorian leaves everything behind and travels to Amsterdam — a city of beauty, secrets, and blurred realities. There, among winding canals and sleepless nights, he encounters strangers who seem to know more about him than they should… and one woman who changes everything.

lilith.

She is warmth in his coldest moments. Light against the creeping dark. For the first time, Dorian begins to believe he can outrun what hunts him

But shadows do not lose their prey.

As his connection with Lilith deepens, the presence that has stalked him all his life grows stronger — more possessive, more violent. Tragedy follows tragedy. Reality fractures. And Dorian is forced to confront a terrifying possibility:

What if the shadow is not chasing him…but trying to take something that isn't meant to be touched.

Now, torn between uncovering the truth and saving the woman he loves, Dorian must decide how far he is willing to go. Because some origins demand sacrifice. And some answers cost everything

Will Dorian uncover the truth behind the darkness that clings to him? Or will his pursuit of salvation drag the woman he loves into an abyss he can never escape?

A haunting tale of love, obsession, and the thin line between fate and free will, this novel explores what happens when the darkness you fear… is the darkness you carry within.
